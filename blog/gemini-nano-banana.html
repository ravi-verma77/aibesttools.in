Gemini Nano Banana (Nano Banana Pro) — Quick Start

This quick guide helps you get started with Gemini Nano Banana (also known as Nano Banana Pro) — a compact, fast image-generation model focused on portrait-style and stylized outputs. Use the banner below as an example output and follow the prompts to create your own variations.

Overview

Gemini Nano Banana is designed for quick, stylized image generation with easy prompt controls. It excels at converting selfies and simple references into watercolor, comic, or stylized portraits with low latency.

When to use it

  • Quick concept art and character portraits.
  • Social media avatars and stylized profile images.
  • Storyboard frames or comic-style illustrations.

Quick Workflow

  1. Choose a reference image or selfie (clear lighting, face visible).
  2. Select the Nano Banana model and desired style (e.g., watercolor, ink, comic).
  3. Add concise style prompt: color palette, mood, and any props.
  4. Generate, then iterate with small prompt tweaks for variation.

Prompt Examples

Try these as starting points — tweak details for your subject.

"Watercolor portrait of a young woman, soft pastels, gentle brush strokes, high detail, warm lighting"

"Comic-style portrait, bold lines, halftone shading, high-contrast colors, dramatic expression"

Tips for Better Results

  • Provide a short example: "like the attached watercolor comic banner".
  • Keep prompts focused—overly long prompts can produce mixed styles.
  • Use iterations: small changes in color, lighting, or facial expression give variation.
  • For commercial use, check the model's licensing and attribution policy.

Safety & Ethics

Respect privacy and consent when editing or generating images of real people. Disclose AI-generated content when appropriate and avoid creating misleading images of public figures.
